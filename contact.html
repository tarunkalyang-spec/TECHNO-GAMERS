Frequently Asked Questions

How do I receive my game key?

After completing your purchase, you'll receive an email with your game key and download instructions within minutes. You can also access your keys in your account dashboard.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and cryptocurrency.

Can I return or refund a game?

Due to the nature of digital products, we cannot offer refunds once the game key has been revealed. However, if you experience technical issues, please contact our support team.

Are the games region-locked?

Most games are region-free, but some may have regional restrictions. We clearly indicate any region locks on the product page before purchase.

How secure is my payment information?

We use industry-standard SSL encryption and PCI-DSS compliant payment processors to ensure your payment information is completely secure.

Do you offer gift cards?

Yes! GameHub gift cards are available in various denominations. They make perfect gifts for gamers and never expire.

What if I have issues with my game key?

If you experience any issues with your game key, contact our 24/7 support team immediately. We'll resolve the issue as quickly as possible.

Do you have a loyalty program?

Yes! Earn points on every purchase that can be redeemed for discounts on future orders. Premium members get exclusive early access to sales.
